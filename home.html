ABOUT ME AND SPINECHILL

When I was a kid I caught my dad watching a movie one afternoon. It was a movie I knew I wasn’t allowed to see “Tremors” starring Kevin Bacon and Fred Ward just to put names up here. It scared the crap out of child me. I was afraid to walk on any ground that wasn’t cement for a few months. Doing full sprints across the lawn of my old house and hopping from one rock to the next hoping they didn't sense me. From that point on though I’ve had an itch and the only way to scratch it is with horror.
Now that I’ve grown up I’ve decided to dedicate a website to some of my favorites from all over the horror genre and medium. Going as far back as silent German films that some find hard to watch but I find fascinating. To the 70’s 80’s slasher eras that I can’t get enough of for some reason. All the way to today’s modern look at deeper fears then what’s just on the surface, which is now scaring the pants of adult me. With everything in between, nothing gives me more of a thrill than finding another good scare to share.
I also love videogames so I”ll also be sharing my love for the horror titles that I’ve played, from my younger days with Resident Evil 2 and 3. All the way to the present while I’m trying to pick which game to play next there’s so many. You’ll hear about all them here, if it passes through the screen into my eyeballs and it’s remotely horror. Be sure you can find it here on Spine Chill.
