ABOUT SPINECHILL

My name is Jason and I love horror. Ever since I was too young to watch, read, and play the things I did it's been something I've been fascinated with. From the good to the bad I love them all and each one played a part in helping me understand my own fears and how to overcome them. Many people say horror is subjective and that's 100% true. But something many people overlook is horror is also an allegory for the real world. Giving many a peak at something that given the true implications of are far more frightening than any make believe monster.
While I may wax poetic at times and do believe that horror is something that should be taken more seriously. This is more about me sharing my love for it and wanting to show you what I see when I consume this content.
